Predicting Medical Insurance Costs

Final Project — Statistical Data Analysis

Analyzed a medical insurance dataset to understand how demographic and lifestyle factors influence healthcare costs. Built and evaluated multiple regression models, identifying smoking and BMI as the dominant cost drivers.

The final model incorporated an interaction between BMI and smoking and achieved an adjusted R² of 0.84, illustrating how lifestyle factors compound financial risk in healthcare.

Exploring the Kindle Marketplace

Final Project — Data Visualization

Analyzed over 130,000 Amazon Kindle book listings to examine trends in pricing, ratings, categories, and participation in Kindle Unlimited. Designed an interactive dashboard to support data-driven publishing and pricing decisions.

Created Power BI visualizations highlighting oversaturated versus emerging categories, publication trends over time, and differences between Kindle Unlimited and non-participating titles.
